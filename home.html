What is Piddly?

Piddly is a payment platform that aims to eliminate the expensive and inconvenient $20 ATM withdrawals by giving consumers the opportunity to use their phone to withdraw $5, $10, or $20 in cash.

  • Sign Up

How it Works

  • You're in need of $5 in cash

  • You log onto the Piddly app and select your bank

  • You fund Piddly account with $5 drafted from debit card

  • Piddly issues you a unique code that you take to a Piddly merchant

  • The merchant verifies your code in the point of sales system

  • Once the code is validated, the cashier gives you the $5

Who it Benefits

  • Consumers

      Saves up to $4.57

    1. No more ATM fees
    2. No more withdrawing excess cash
    3. Eliminates the hassle of breaking a $20 for smaller cash denominations

  • Small Banks

      Saves up to $6.50

    1. Eliminates ATM reimbursement fees
    2. Eliminates reverse ATM interchange fees
    3. Increases interchange revenue
    4. Reduces cash usage

  • Big Banks

      Saves up to $1.75

    1. Increases interchange revenue
    2. Eliminates reverse ATM interchange fees
    3. Reduces cash usage

  • Credit Card Networks

      Saves up to 5 swipe fee transactions

    1. Increases interchange revenue
    2. Reduces cash usage
