What is Piddly?

Piddly is a payment platform that aims to eliminate the expensive and inconvenient $20 ATM withdrawals by giving consumers the opportunity to use their phone to withdraw $5, $10, or $20 in cash.

  • Sign Up

How it Works

  • Link You need to get
    $5 in cash.
  • Gavel You log onto the Piddly app and select your bank.
  • User You fund Piddly account with $5 drafted from checking.
  • Plane Piddly issues you a unique code that you take to a Piddly merchant.
  • Rocket The merchant verifies your code in the point of sales system.
  • Cog Once the code is validated, the cashier gives you the $5.

Who it Benefits

  • Consumers

    • No longer have to pay huge ATM fees.
    • No longer need to withdraw the $20 ATM minimum
    • Gives you the opportunity to withdraw $5 or $10.
    • Saves you time and money from having to break a $20 bill because of the $20 ATM minimum.
    • Reduces your cash usage.
    • Reduces ATM card skimming.

  • Small Banks

    • Eliminates the need to reimburse ATM surcharge fees.
    • Eliminates the need to pay interchange fees to the ATM owner.
    • Gives your customers an opportunity to withdraw $5 or $10.
